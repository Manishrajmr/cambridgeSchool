Admissions Policy

Admission Policy and Process: Academic Session 2022-23
Admissions open each year for classes from Nursery to IX and XI.
Admission is merit-based and subject to the availability of seats. There is a provision for daycare facilities to be availed of based on a fee related to usage.
Maximum admissions are done at the nursery level based on the interaction with the child and the parents.
Admissions to classes above this entry-level depend upon the vacancy of seats in the concerned class, the performance of applicant children in written tests, and oral interactions with students and parents. Provisional admission granted to students in Class XI prior to the declaration of Board exam results is confirmed on the basis of Class X results declared by the CBSE.
The application form and prospectus can be collected from the Cambridge School, Noida office, on payment of Rs. 500/-.
The application form, duly completed in all respects and accompanied by the necessary documents as mentioned in the application form, should be submitted by the date mentioned on the website.
The submission of an application form or its acceptance by the school office does not in any way guarantee admission.
Appearing for an oral test, written test, or interaction with the school staff and principal does not guarantee admission to the school.
The requisite admission formalities, including payment of fees, are required to be completed within 3 working days of the offer of admission.
Students applying under the EWS category should get in touch with the DIOS, Noida (U.P.), as per the applicable rules.
Parents seeking withdrawal of their ward after payment of fees shall be eligible for reimbursement as follows:
If a child is withdrawn prior to attending school, only registration and admission fees will be deducted from the fees paid, and the rest will be refunded.
If a child is withdrawn after joining the school and attending class, parents may either serve one month’s notice in writing or pay one month’s fee in lieu thereof.
Admission Policy for Class XI: Admission to class XI will be granted based on the following:
Results of Class IX (Mid Term and Final Term) and Class X (Mid Term and Post mid-term) will be considered if the Board result is not declared.
Result of the Aptitude Test (Stream Selection Test) conducted by the school.
